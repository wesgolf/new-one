Future roadmap for Muse / Artist OS

Build the app as a modular artist operating system with four long-term product pillars:

1. Growth

* Smart Links & Pre-Saves
* Fan CRM & Email
* Playlist Pitch Agent
* Competitor Analysis

2. Live Career

* Venue Booking
* Electronic Press Kit

3. Analytics & Intelligence

* Artist Analytics (including Spotify and other supported platforms)
* Competitor Benchmarking
* Reporting for managers, labels, and collaborators

4. Content Operations

* Social Scheduling
* Platform-specific publishing settings
* Best posting time recommendations
* Audio workflow automation where relevant

Future modules to support:

Venue Booking

* Let the user define target cities, venues, and show goals
* Research venues and store structured venue records
* Track capacity, genre fit, city, contact info, notes, and booking history
* Draft personalized outreach pitches
* Track sent, replied, follow-up, booked, and declined statuses

Fan CRM & Email

* Capture fans from pre-saves, smart links, website forms, and signups
* Store fan profiles and source attribution
* Segment fans by location, engagement, and source
* Send campaigns for releases, shows, and announcements
* Track campaign history and engagement

Electronic Press Kit

* Generate a shareable public EPK page
* Include bio, press photos, music links, stats, embedded content, and rider information
* Make it easy to update without rebuilding the page each time

Smart Links & Pre-Saves

* Create branded release links and pre-save campaigns
* Support multiple platforms in one link
* Track clicks, conversions, and source performance
* Integrate with the fan CRM where possible

Artist Analytics

* Show key artist metrics in one place, including streaming, playlist, social, and audience data where available
* Sync data regularly
* Avoid building this as Spotify-only if the underlying providers support broader coverage

Playlist Pitch Agent

* Research playlist curators and relevant contacts
* Generate personalized pitch drafts
* Track outreach, opens, replies, and follow-ups
* Keep the user in control before sending anything

Competitor Analysis

* Track selected comparable artists
* Benchmark growth, releases, playlist traction, and platform performance
* Surface trends and opportunities in a clear dashboard

Reporting

* Generate clean reports for managers, labels, venues, or investors
* Include campaign performance, audience growth, content performance, and release metrics
* Support exportable summaries and charts

Potential integrations

* Use Auphonic for audio processing and workflow automation where relevant
* Evaluate Soundcharts for music metadata, artist intelligence, venue/festival data, and competitor research
* Evaluate Songstats for artist, label, and track analytics, especially for reporting and performance dashboards

Architecture requirements

* Keep each module separate and extensible
* Use Supabase as the system of record where possible
* Store third-party sync results in normalized tables
* Use clear service layers/adapters for external APIs
* Make it easy to enable modules over time without rewriting the app
